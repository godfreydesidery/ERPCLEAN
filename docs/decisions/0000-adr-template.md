## Context

What forces the decision? What constraints exist? What did we already try or rule out? This is the section a future engineer reads first to understand whether the decision still applies.

## Decision

What did we choose? State it plainly in one or two sentences.

## Consequences

What changes as a result — positive, negative, neutral. Include things that become harder, not just things that become easier.

## Alternatives considered

What else was on the table? For each: one sentence on why it was rejected.
